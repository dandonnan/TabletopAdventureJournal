Search Queries

It is possible to filter searches by adding queries to the end of the search.

Quests
&complete   - Filter by completed quests
&incomplete   - Filter by incomplete quests

Characters
&party   - Filter by characters in the party
&nonparty   - Filter by characters not in the party
&dead   - Filter by characters that are dead
&alive   - Filter by characters that are alive
